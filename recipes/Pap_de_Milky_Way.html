Pap de Milky Way

Description

This porridge recipe is lightweight, easy to pack, and can be prepared quickly with hot water. It provides a good source of protein, fibre, and carbohydrates to keep astronauts fueled and satisfied during their space travels.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup instant oats
  • 1/2 cup powdered milk
  • 1/4 cup raisins or other dried fruit
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (such as almonds or walnuts)
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a resealable bag and mix well.
  2. When ready to eat, add hot water to the bag and stir until desired consistency is reached.
  3. Enjoy with a spoon or directly from the bag.
