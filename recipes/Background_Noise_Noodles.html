Background Noise Noodles

Description

Not only visually striking, these background-noise noodles are packed with flavour and nutrients. The soybeans provide a good source of protein, while the seasonings add a spicy whump to the dish. The angry red noodleness adds a galactic glow that could light up any command module in low-power mode.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of red noodles
  • 1 can soy beans, drained
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon of red-hot spices
  • 1/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 1 teaspoon sriracha sauce

Directions

  1. Cook the red noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. In a small saucepan, combine the soybeans, sriracha sauce and black pepper. Cook over medium heat for 5-10 minutes, until heated through and well combined.
  3. Serve the noodles in a bowl and top with chopped green onions.
