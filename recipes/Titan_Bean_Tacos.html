Titan Bean Tacos

Description

This recipe is easy to prepare, requires minimal cooking equipment, and provides a good balance of carbohydrates, protein, and healthy fats. It's also customizable, so astronauts can adjust the ingredients to their preferences and dietary restrictions.

Ingredients

  • 1 can black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 can corn, drained
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 4 tortillas
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1 avocado, sliced

Directions

  1. In a small saucepan, combine the black beans, corn and chili powder. Cook over medium heat for 5-10 minutes, until heated through and well combined.
  2. Assemble the tacos by placing a spoonful of the bean mixture on each tortilla, then topping with salsa, and avocado slices.
  3. Fold the tortillas in half and enjoy.
